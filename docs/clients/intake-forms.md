# Intake Forms

Digital intake forms let clients complete paperwork online before their first session.

## Assigning Intake Forms

1. Open a client's profile
2. Go to the **Intake Forms** tab
3. Click **Assign Forms**
4. Select the forms you want the client to complete
5. The forms become available in the client's portal

## Client Experience

1. Client receives portal access (via email invite)
2. They log in and see their assigned forms
3. They complete and submit each form
4. You receive a notification when all forms are completed

## Viewing Completed Forms

1. Go to the client's **Intake Forms** tab
2. Click on any completed form to view responses
3. Responses are stored securely and linked to the client's record
