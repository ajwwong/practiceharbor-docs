# Adding a Client

## From the Clients Page

1. Go to **Clients** in the navigation
2. Click **Add Client**
3. Enter the client's **name**, **date of birth**, and **contact information**
4. Click **Save**

## From the Calendar

1. Click on a time slot to create a new appointment
2. In the client selector, click **Create New Client**
3. Enter the client's basic information
4. The client is created and the appointment is saved

## Client Profile

After adding a client, their profile page includes:

- **Dashboard** — upcoming appointments, billing summary, and recent notes
- **Intake Forms** — assigned intake questionnaires and their status
- **Documents** — uploaded files and documents
- **Billing** — invoices, payments, and billing history

To edit client details (demographics, contacts, portal access), click **Edit** on the client's profile to access:

- **Client Info** — name, contact info, diagnoses
- **Billing** — rates, insurance, billing preferences
- **Contacts** — guardians, emergency contacts, related persons
- **Portal** — client portal access and invitations
