# Client Portal

The client portal gives your clients a secure place to view appointments, complete intake forms, send messages, and manage their information.

## Inviting a Client to the Portal

1. Open the client's profile and click **Edit**
2. Go to the **Portal** tab
3. Click **Send Portal Invitation**
4. The client receives an email with a link to create their portal account

## What Clients Can Do

- **View appointments** — see upcoming and past sessions
- **Complete intake forms** — fill out assigned questionnaires
- **Send secure messages** — HIPAA-compliant messaging with their provider
- **Request appointments** — request new session times (if online booking is enabled)
- **View documents** — access shared documents

## Multi-Practice Support

If a client sees providers at multiple practices using the platform, they use a single portal login to access all of their practices.

## Contact/Guardian Portal Access

For minor clients, guardians and contacts can also receive portal access:

1. Open the client's profile, click **Edit**, then go to the **Contacts** tab
2. Add a contact (parent, guardian, etc.)
3. Enable **Portal Access** for that contact
4. The contact can view the client's appointments and complete forms on their behalf
