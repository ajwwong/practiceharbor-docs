# Contacts & Guardians

Add contacts like parents, guardians, emergency contacts, or other related persons to a client's profile.

## Adding a Contact

1. Open the client's profile and click **Edit**
2. Go to the **Contacts** tab
3. Click **Add Contact**
4. Enter the contact's name, relationship, phone, and email
5. Choose permissions:
   - **Portal access** — can the contact log into the portal for this client?
   - **Document sharing** — can they view shared documents?
6. Click **Save**

## Use Cases

- **Minor clients** — add parents/guardians who need portal access and form completion
- **Emergency contacts** — document who to contact in an emergency
- **Couples therapy** — link partners as contacts on each other's profiles
- **Family therapy** — add family members involved in treatment

## Managing Contact Permissions

- Toggle portal access on/off at any time from the Contacts tab
- Revoke access instantly if needed
