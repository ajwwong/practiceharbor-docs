# Online Booking

Online booking lets clients request appointments through the client portal. You review and approve each request.

## How It Works

1. A client logs into the **Client Portal**
2. They click **Request Appointment**
3. They select a preferred date and time
4. You receive a notification and review the request
5. You **approve** or **decline** the request
6. The client is notified of your decision

## Setting Up Online Booking

1. Go to **Settings > Online Booking**
2. Configure your availability and preferences
3. Enable booking for your client portal

:::tip
Online booking uses a review-and-approve model — clients request times, but you always have the final say. No surprise bookings.
:::
