# Google Calendar Sync

Two-way Google Calendar sync keeps your schedule in sync across both platforms.

## What Syncs

- **From Google → here**: Google Calendar events show as blocked time on your calendar (prevents double-booking)
- **From here → Google**: Appointments you create here appear on your Google Calendar

## Setting Up Sync

1. Go to **Settings > Calendar**
2. Click **Connect Google Calendar**
3. Sign in with your Google account and grant calendar access
4. Choose which Google calendar to sync with
5. Sync starts automatically

## Important Notes

- Client names and session details are **not** sent to Google — only the time block and a generic title (for HIPAA compliance)
- Google events appear as "busy" blocks on your calendar
- Sync happens in real time

## Disconnecting

1. Go to **Settings > Calendar**
2. Click **Disconnect Google Calendar**
