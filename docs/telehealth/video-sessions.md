# Video Sessions

Conduct HIPAA-compliant video sessions directly in the browser. No downloads required for you or your client.

## Starting a Video Session

1. Create an appointment for the client (or use an existing one)
2. At the scheduled time, click **Join Session** on the appointment
3. Your client receives a link to join (via email reminder or client portal)
4. Both of you connect via browser — no app download needed

## During the Session

- **Video and audio** — full two-way video and audio
- **AI recording** — if enabled, the session audio is transcribed for AI note generation
- **Screen sharing** — share your screen if needed

## Technical Requirements

- A modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A working microphone and camera
- A stable internet connection

:::tip
For the best experience, use **Chrome** and close unnecessary browser tabs to free up bandwidth.
:::

## Recording Telehealth Sessions

When you start a telehealth session with recording enabled, the AI captures both audio streams and generates a progress note when the session ends — just like an in-person recorded session.
