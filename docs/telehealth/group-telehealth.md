# Group Telehealth

Run couples and family therapy sessions over video with multiple participants.

## Starting a Group Session

1. Create an appointment for a **couple or family group**
2. At the scheduled time, click **Join Session**
3. Each group member receives their own join link
4. All participants connect via browser

## AI Notes for Group Sessions

- The AI captures all participants' audio
- Notes are generated using your selected group template (Couples Therapy, Family Therapy)
- The AI identifies different speakers and captures group dynamics

## Requirements

- Available on the **Plus** plan
- Each participant needs their own device with a browser, microphone, and camera
