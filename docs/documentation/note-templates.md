# Note Templates

Progress notes are generated using templates that define the structure of your documentation. You can use built-in templates or create your own.

## Standard Templates

The platform includes 13 standard templates organized by category:

**Therapy Notes**
- Therapy Intake
- Follow-up Therapy
- Couples Therapy
- Family Therapy
- Group Therapy

**Clinical Formats**
- SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan)
- DAP (Data, Assessment, Plan)
- BIRP (Behavior, Intervention, Response, Plan)
- GIRP (Goals, Intervention, Response, Plan)
- PIRP (Problem, Intervention, Response, Plan)

**Specialty**
- Supervision Note
- Discharge Summary
- Crisis Note

## Setting Your Default Template

1. Go to **Settings > Note Preferences**
2. Choose your preferred default template
3. This template will be used automatically when generating AI notes

## Creating Custom Templates

1. Go to **Templates** in the navigation
2. Click the **My Library** tab
3. Click **Create Template**
4. Define your template sections (e.g., "Presenting Problem", "Interventions", "Client Response")
5. Save your template

:::tip
Custom templates let you match your practice's specific documentation requirements or insurance expectations.
:::

## Gallery

Browse all available templates in the **Gallery** tab:
1. Go to **Templates**
2. Click the **Gallery** tab
3. Click on any template to preview its sections
4. Click **Add to Library** to add it to your personal collection
