# Treatment Plans

Treatment plans help you track clinical goals, interventions, and client progress over time.

## Creating a Treatment Plan

1. Open a client's profile
2. Click **Treatment Plan** in the client's menu or navigate to their treatment plan page
3. Click **Create Treatment Plan**
4. Add:
   - **Diagnosis** — select from ICD-10 codes
   - **Goals** — what the client is working toward
   - **Objectives** — measurable steps toward each goal
   - **Interventions** — therapeutic approaches you'll use
5. Save the treatment plan

## Updating Progress

- Update goal status as the client progresses (e.g., "In Progress", "Achieved")
- Add notes on progress toward each objective
- Treatment plan updates are tracked over time

## Using Treatment Plans with AI Notes

When you record a session, the AI can reference the client's active treatment plan to ensure your note aligns with documented goals and interventions.
