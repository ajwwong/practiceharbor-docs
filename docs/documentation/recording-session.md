# Recording a Session

You can record sessions in two ways: **Live Session** (real-time during a telehealth call) or **Dictation** (record after the session or in-person).

## Live Session (Telehealth)

1. Start a telehealth video session with your client
2. The recording captures both audio streams automatically
3. When the session ends, the AI generates your note

## Dictation (In-Person or After Session)

1. Click the **microphone icon** in the navigation bar
2. Select the **client** for this session
3. Click **Start Recording**
4. Speak naturally — summarize what happened in the session, or dictate your note directly
5. Click **End Session**
6. The AI generates a structured note from your dictation

## Tips for Better Notes

- **Speak naturally** — you don't need to follow a specific format; the AI will structure it
- **Include key details** — mention interventions used, client responses, mood/affect observations, and plan for next session
- **Use your normal clinical language** — the AI understands clinical terminology

## Session Limits

- **Free**: Includes AI notes (limited sessions)
- **Essential**: 100 AI sessions per month
- **Plus**: Unlimited AI sessions

Your remaining sessions are shown on the recording page when you're running low.
