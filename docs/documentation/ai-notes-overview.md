# How AI Notes Work

AI-powered notes let you focus on your client while the platform handles documentation. Here's how it works.

## The Process

1. **You record** — start a recording during your session (in-person or telehealth)
2. **AI transcribes** — the audio is securely transcribed in real time
3. **AI drafts your note** — using your selected template (SOAP, DAP, BIRP, etc.), the AI generates a structured progress note from the transcript
4. **You review and sign** — edit anything that needs adjustment, then sign the note

## What the AI Does Well

- Captures key clinical details from the session
- Structures content into your chosen note format
- Identifies relevant interventions, client responses, and themes
- Saves you 15-30 minutes per note

## What You Should Always Review

- **Clinical accuracy** — the AI captures what was said, but your clinical judgment determines what's documented
- **Diagnostic impressions** — verify any referenced diagnoses or assessments
- **Plan details** — confirm follow-up plans and next session details

:::tip
Think of the AI as a very fast scribe, not a replacement for your clinical judgment. It drafts — you decide what stays.
:::

## Privacy & Security

- All audio is processed in a HIPAA-compliant environment
- Transcription data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Your BAA covers all AI processing
- No audio or transcript data is used to train AI models
