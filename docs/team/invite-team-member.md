# Invite a Team Member

Add other clinicians, supervisors, or administrative staff to your practice.

## Sending an Invite

1. Go to **Settings > Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their **name** and **email**
4. Select their **role** (see below)
5. Click **Send Invitation**
6. They receive an email to set their password and join your practice

## Roles

### Clinician Roles
- **Entire Practice** — full access to all clients, notes, and settings
- **Full Client List** — can view all clients but only edit their own
- **Basic** — can only see and manage their own clients

### Administrative Roles
- **Practice Manager** — full administrative access without clinical permissions
- **Practice Biller** — access to billing and payment features
- **Practice Scheduler** — access to scheduling and calendar only
