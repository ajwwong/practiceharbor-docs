# Supervision

The supervision workflow lets supervisees share notes for review and supervisors co-sign them.

## For Supervisees

1. Write or generate your progress note
2. Click **Submit for Review** to send it to your supervisor
3. The note appears in your supervisor's review queue
4. Once co-signed, the note is finalized

## For Supervisors

1. Go to the **Supervision** page (in the navigation)
2. Review notes submitted by your supervisees
3. Read the note and provide feedback if needed
4. Click **Co-Sign Note** to add your digital signature
5. The supervisee is notified that the note has been co-signed

## Setting Up Supervision

When inviting a team member, designate them as a **supervisor**. Supervisors automatically get access to the supervision dashboard and review queue.
