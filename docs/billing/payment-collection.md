# Collecting Payments

Multiple ways to collect payment from clients.

## Charge a Saved Card

1. Open the client's **Billing** tab
2. Select an unpaid invoice
3. Click **Add Payment**
4. Choose the saved card on file
5. Confirm the charge

## Enter a New Card

1. Click **Add Payment** on an invoice
2. Select **New Card**
3. Enter the card details (processed securely via Stripe)
4. Optionally check **Save card for future use**
5. Confirm the charge

## Record a Cash or Check Payment

1. Click **Add Payment** on an invoice
2. Select **Cash** or **Check**
3. Enter the amount received
4. The invoice is marked as paid

## Collecting a Card During Intake

You can collect a payment method during the client intake process:
1. When setting up intake forms, enable the **Payment Method** step
2. The client enters their card information during onboarding
3. The card is saved securely for future billing
