# Superbills

Superbills are detailed receipts that clients can submit to their insurance company for out-of-network reimbursement.

## What's on a Superbill

- Provider information (name, NPI, Tax ID)
- Client information
- Date of service
- CPT codes (procedure codes)
- ICD-10 codes (diagnosis codes)
- Amount charged and paid

## Creating a Superbill

1. Go to a client's **Billing** tab
2. Click **Create Superbill**
3. Select the billing items to include
4. A PDF superbill is created with all required fields

## Sending to Clients

- On any billing item, click the menu and select **Send Superbill** to email the PDF
- You can also click **Download Superbill PDF** to save it locally
