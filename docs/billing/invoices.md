# Invoices

Create invoices for sessions and services, and send them to clients via email.

## Automatic Invoices

Invoices are created automatically after each session based on your billing settings:
- The service rate is determined by your rate hierarchy (service > client > practitioner > organization)
- Invoices appear in the **Billing** section

## Manual Invoice Creation

1. Go to a client's **Billing** tab (or the **Billing** page in the navigation)
2. Click **Create Invoice**
3. Select the service, date, and amount
4. Save the invoice

## Sending Invoices

1. Open an invoice
2. Click **Send via Email**
3. The client receives a PDF invoice in their email

## Invoice Statuses

- **Issued** — invoice created and sent to the client
- **Balanced** — payment received in full
- **Cancelled** — invoice voided
