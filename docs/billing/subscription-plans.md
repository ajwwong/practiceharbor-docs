# Subscription Plans

Choose the plan that fits your practice.

## Plans

### Free ($0/month)
Everything you need to get started:
- Unlimited clients
- Telehealth (video sessions)
- AI-powered progress notes
- Client portal
- Scheduling & reminders
- Online booking
- Billing & superbills
- Intake forms
- Secure messaging

### Essential ($9/month)
Everything in Free, plus:
- 100 AI sessions per month
- 2-way Google Calendar sync
- Treatment plans
- Data export
- Supervision workflow

### Plus ($19/month) — Most Popular
Everything in Essential, plus:
- Unlimited AI sessions
- Group telehealth (couples/family)
- Group scheduling & notes
- EHR import
- Team members & roles

## Changing Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Select the plan you want
3. Enter your payment information (for paid plans)
4. Your new plan takes effect immediately

## Referral Program

Refer a colleague and both of you get 2 free months when they make their second payment. Share your referral link from the app.
