# AutoPay

AutoPay automatically charges clients using their saved credit or debit card after each session. No manual invoicing needed.

## How It Works

1. A client saves a payment method (during intake or manually)
2. You enable AutoPay for that client
3. After each session, the platform automatically charges the saved card
4. Both you and the client receive a confirmation

## Setting Up AutoPay

### For Individual Clients

1. Go to the client's **Billing** tab
2. Click **Enable AutoPay**
3. Confirm the saved payment method

### Bulk Enrollment

1. Go to **Settings > AutoPay**
2. See all clients with saved payment methods
3. Use **Select All** to enable AutoPay for multiple clients at once

## Couples/Family Billing

For couples and family therapy, AutoPay charges the **guarantor** (responsible party) automatically. The guarantor's card is used for group session billing.

## FSA/HSA Cards

AutoPay works with FSA and HSA cards. Clients can save these cards as their payment method.
