# Practice Settings

Manage your practice's organizational details.

## Updating Practice Information

1. Go to **Settings > Practice Details**
2. Update:
   - **Practice name**
   - **Address**
   - **Phone number**
   - **Website**
3. Click **Save**

This information appears on invoices, superbills, and other billing documents.
