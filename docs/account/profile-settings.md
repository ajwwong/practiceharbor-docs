# Profile Settings

Manage your personal and professional information.

## Updating Your Profile

1. Go to **Settings > Profile**
2. Update any of the following:
   - **Name** and **credentials** (e.g., PhD, LCSW, LPC)
   - **NPI number**
   - **Tax ID**
   - **Profile photo**
3. Click **Save**

## Note Preferences

Customize how your AI notes are generated:

1. Go to **Settings > Note Preferences**
2. Set your **default note template** (SOAP, DAP, BIRP, etc.)
3. Configure any template-specific preferences

## Calendar Preferences

1. Go to **Settings > Calendar**
2. Set your **timezone**
3. Connect **Google Calendar** for two-way sync
4. Configure your default appointment duration
