# Your First Session

This guide walks you through recording your first therapy session and generating an AI progress note.

## Before You Start

1. **Add a client** — go to the Clients page and click **Add Client** (or use the quick-add from the calendar)
2. **Choose a note template** — go to Settings > Note Preferences to pick your default template (SOAP, DAP, BIRP, or others)
3. **Test your microphone** — make sure your browser has microphone access

## Record a Session

1. Navigate to the **session recording page** (click the microphone icon in the nav, or start from an appointment)
2. Select the **client** for this session
3. Click **Start Recording**
4. Conduct your session as normal — the AI listens and transcribes in the background
5. When finished, click **End Session**

## Generate Your Note

After recording:

1. The AI generates a draft progress note using your selected template
2. Review the draft — it includes the key clinical details from your session
3. Use **Magic Edit** to refine any section (e.g., "make the assessment more detailed")
4. Click **Sign Note** when you're satisfied (or **Submit for Review** if you're under supervision)

:::tip
The AI drafts the note, but you're always in control. Review every note before signing — it's your clinical judgment that matters.
:::

## What Happens to the Recording?

- The audio is processed securely in a HIPAA-compliant environment
- Transcription is used only to generate your note
- You can choose your retention preferences in Settings
