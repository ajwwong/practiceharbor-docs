# Set Your Password

After signing up, you'll receive an email with a link to set your password and activate your account.

## Steps

1. Check your email for a welcome message from us
2. Click the **Set Password** link in the email
3. Choose a strong password that includes:
   - At least 8 characters
   - An uppercase letter
   - A lowercase letter
   - A number
   - A special character
4. Click **Set Password**
5. You'll be redirected to the sign-in page

:::info Don't see the email?
- Check your **spam or junk folder**
- Make sure you entered the correct email address during signup
- If you still can't find it, go to the sign-in page and click **Forgot password** to request a new link
:::

## Signing In

Once your password is set:

1. Go to the sign-in page (click **Back to App** in the top menu)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign in**

## Forgot Your Password?

1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Click **Forgot password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link
