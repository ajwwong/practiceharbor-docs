# Create Your Account

Getting started takes about 2 minutes. No credit card required.

## How to Sign Up

1. Click **Back to App** in the top menu, then click **Register** (or go to the registration page directly)
2. Enter your **first name**, **last name**, and **email address**
3. Enter your **practice or organization name**
4. Click **Start my free trial**
5. Check your email for a link to set your password

:::tip
By signing up, you automatically agree to our Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Business Associate Agreement (BAA) — no extra paperwork needed.
:::

## What's Included

Your free account includes:
- **Unlimited clients** — no cap on how many clients you manage
- **Telehealth** — browser-based video sessions
- **AI-powered notes** — voice-to-text transcription and note generation
- **Scheduling** — calendar with email reminders
- **Billing** — invoices, superbills, and online payments
- **Client portal** — secure portal for your clients
- **Intake forms** — digital intake and consent forms
- **Secure messaging** — HIPAA-compliant provider-client messaging
